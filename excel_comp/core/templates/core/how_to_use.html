Introduction

How to use

  1. Go to the file upload page.
  2. Drag your document or click browse and upload your document on the squared space.
  3. Click on compare to compare the information in your document.
  4. View the results and click the download option to save the document on your device.
  5. All documents are automatically saved in the system and can be viewed in the history tab of your profile.
