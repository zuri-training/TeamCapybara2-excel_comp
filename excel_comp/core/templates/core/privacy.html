PRIVACY POLICY

Introduction

At Excel_Comp , we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This privacy policy applies to the information collected on our website, and explains how we, collect, use and share that information.

We may also collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, such as your name, email address, and other contact information.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information we collect to operate and improve our website and the services we offer. This may include using your information to personalize your experience on our website, to communicate with you, or to analyze usage patterns and trends.

We may also use your information for research and development purposes, to ensure the security of our website and services, or to comply with legal obligations.

Sharing your Information

We will not sell or rent your personal Information to third parties without your consent. However, we may share your information with certain service providers who assist us in operating our website or providing our services.

We may also disclose your information as required by law , such as to comply with subpoena or other legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Excel_Comp, our users, or others.

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, altercation, and destruction. However, no internet or email transmission is ever fully secure or error-free, and you should take special care in deciding what information you send to us.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes to our information practices. We encourage you to periodically review this page for latest information on our privacy practices.
